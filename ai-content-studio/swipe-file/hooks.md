# Hook Library

Proven hook patterns organized by category. Use these to open LinkedIn posts, emails, and blog introductions.

## Question Hooks
- "What if you could [benefit] without [pain]?"
- "How long does it take your team to [task]?"
- "Why do most [industry] teams still [common mistake]?"
- "What happens when [common practice] stops working?"
- "When was the last time your team [desired behavior]?"

## Statistic Hooks
- "We analyzed [N] [topic] and found [surprising pattern]."
- "[Number]% of [audience] still [problem]. Here's the fix."
- "Most [industry] teams get [X] wrong. Here's why."
- "[Number] hours wasted per [time period] on [task]. Unacceptable."
- "The average [role] spends [time] on [task]. We got it down to [time]."

## Bold Claim Hooks
- "Stop doing [common practice]. Do this instead."
- "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's the data."
- "The best [teams] don't [conventional approach]. They do this."
- "Everything you know about [topic] is about to change."
- "Here's the [topic] truth no one wants to admit."

## Story Hooks
- "We watched [persona] try to [task]. It took [time]. We fixed it."
- "A [role] told me: '[quote]'. That changed how we think about [topic]."
- "Last week, a [persona] showed me [insight]. I couldn't unsee it."
- "I spent [time] doing [task]. Then I realized [insight]."
- "[Persona] came to us with [problem]. Here's what happened next."

## Contrarian Hooks
- "Why I stopped believing in [common wisdom]"
- "The [topic] metric everyone tracks is the wrong one."
- "[Common practice] is making your [outcome] worse."
- "We tried [popular approach]. It failed. Here's what worked."
- "Everyone's talking about [trend]. Here's why I'm skeptical."

## Emotional Hooks
- "That feeling when [pain point]? We built the fix."
- "If [pain point] sounds familiar, this is for you."
- "The most expensive mistake in [industry]? [Specific mistake]."
- "I've never seen [audience] so excited about [topic]."
- "This [result] made our team emotional. Here's why."
