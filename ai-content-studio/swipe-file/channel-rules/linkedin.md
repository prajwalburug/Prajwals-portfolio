# LinkedIn Channel Rules

## Character Count
- Target: 900-1500 characters (sweet spot for engagement)
- Short posts: 300-600 characters (high urgency or announcements)
- Long posts: 1500-2500 characters (storytelling or deep dives)
- Hard limit: 3000 characters

## Hook
- Must land in first 2 lines (visible without "see more")
- Types: Question, bold claim, statistic, short story, contrarian take
- No generic openings ("I'm excited to share...")

## Formatting
- One idea per paragraph
- Line break between every 2-3 sentences
- No walls of text
- Use **bold** for emphasis on key numbers or phrases

## Hashtags
- 3-5 hashtags
- All lowercase
- Place at the very end
- Mix: 2 broad (#hiring, #ai) + 2 specific (#hrtech, #startups) + 1 brand (#awesomehires)

## CTA
- Ask a question to invite comments
- "Tag someone who needs to see this"
- "What's your take?"
- No hard sales CTAs on organic posts

## Timing
- Best times: Tue-Thu 8-10am EST
- Worst: Weekends, late Friday

## Post Types
- Thought leadership (opinion + data point)
- Story (personal anecdote → lesson learned)
- Educational (how-to, framework, checklist)
- Product (announcement, feature launch — 1 in 10 posts max)
- Engagement (poll, question, hot take)
