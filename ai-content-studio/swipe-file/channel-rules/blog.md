# Blog Channel Rules

## Structure
- Title: 50-60 characters, includes primary keyword
- Meta description: 150-160 characters
- URL slug: lowercase, hyphens, keyword
- H2 every ~300 words
- H3 for subsections under H2

## First 300 Words
- Data point or statistic in first 150 words
- State the problem immediately
- Tell them what they'll learn (thesis statement)
- No fluff introductions

## Content
- One idea per H2 section
- Support claims with data, sources, or named examples
- Include at least one internal link to related content
- Include at least one external link to authoritative source
- Bullet points or numbered lists for frameworks/steps

## Length
- Short: 800-1200 words (tips, announcements)
- Medium: 1500-2500 words (how-to, thought leadership)
- Long: 3000+ words (ultimate guides, research reports)

## Images
- One image every 2-3 sections
- Featured image: 1200x628px (OG standard)
- Alt text: descriptive, includes keyword where natural
- Image type: screenshots, data visualizations, illustrations

## SEO
- Primary keyword in: title, H1, first 150 words, one H2, meta description, URL
- Secondary keywords in: H2s, body text naturally
- Internal links: 2-3 per post
- External links: 1-2 to authoritative sources

## CTA Placement
- Bottom of post: primary CTA (demo, trial)
- Mid-post: contextual CTA (related resource)
- Sidebar/sticky: secondary CTA (newsletter)
