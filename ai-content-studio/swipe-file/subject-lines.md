# Subject Line Library

High-performing subject line patterns organized by email intent.

## Cold Outreach
- "30-minute candidate shortlist?"
- "[Company]'s hiring process — one change"
- "Your team vs. scheduling calls"
- "Quick question about your hiring pipeline"
- "[Number] hours your team wastes on scheduling"
- "Faster hiring for [company]?"
- "The [role] you're looking for — a faster path"
- "Fair interviews, no scheduling"
- "Your [company] hiring bottleneck"
- "A better way to screen candidates"

## Newsletter
- "[Number] ways to [benefit] without [pain]"
- "Your weekly [topic] roundup"
- "The [X] playbook for [audience]"
- "This week in [industry]: [key insight]"
- "5-minute read: [topic]"
- "What we learned about [topic] this month"
- "[Industry] insider: [key trend]"
- "The [number] things every [role] should know about [topic]"
- "Fresh thinking on [topic]"
- "[Topic]: What's working right now"

## Re-engagement
- "Still thinking about [topic]?"
- "Did we lose you?"
- "One more thing about [topic]"
- "I wanted to circle back on [topic]"
- "Should I take you off this list?"
- "Last chance: [offer/value]"
- "We haven't spoken in a while"
- "[Topic] — worth another look?"
- "Coming back to this..."
- "A quick [timeframe] follow-up"

## Event / Webinar
- "Join us: [event name]"
- "Invite: [topic] webinar"
- "See [product] in action — [date]"
- "Your seat is saved for [event]"
- "Can't make it? Here's the recording"

## Product Announcement
- "Meet [feature name]"
- "We just shipped [announcement]"
- "Big update: [what changed]"
- "New: [feature] is live"
- "[Product] just got [benefit]"
